What We Do

All Aircon Types,
One Trusted Team

We clean and maintain every major aircon type — fully equipped, done right.

Split Type

Deep cleaning of filters, evaporator coil, fan blower, and drainage. Restores cooling and eliminates bacteria.

Window Type

Full disassembly, coil washing, fan blade cleaning, and reassembly. Quiet and efficient after every service.

Cassette Type

Specialized cleaning for ceiling-mounted units. All vanes, coil, and drain pan — no mess, no damage.

Floor Mounted
